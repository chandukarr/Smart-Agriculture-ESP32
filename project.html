Project Overview

This project presents a Smart Agriculture and Safety System developed using the ESP32 microcontroller. It automates irrigation based on soil moisture, monitors environmental conditions such as temperature and humidity, tracks water tank levels, and enhances field security through motion detection.

Objectives

Monitor soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and water level in real time.
Automate irrigation to reduce water wastage.
Detect human or animal intrusion for field safety.
Display live sensor data for easy monitoring.

Functional Description

1. Soil Moisture Monitoring and Irrigation Automation

  • The soil moisture sensor continuously measures soil dryness.
  • If the moisture level drops below a predefined threshold, the ESP32 activates the relay.
  • The relay switches ON the water pump, automatically irrigating the crops.
  • Once sufficient moisture is detected, the pump turns OFF to prevent water wastage.
  • Soil moisture values and pump status are displayed on the LCD.

2. Environmental Monitoring

  • The DHT11 sensor measures temperature and humidity at regular intervals.
  • The ESP32 processes these values and displays them on the LCD.
  • This data helps farmers understand field conditions and crop health.

3. Water Level Monitoring

  • The ultrasonic sensor measures the distance between the tank top and the water surface.
  • Green LED glows when the water level is sufficient.
  • Red LED glows when the water level is low.
  • This provides instant visual feedback for timely water refilling.

4. Security and Intrusion Detection

  • The PIR sensor continuously monitors movement in the field.
  • When motion is detected, the ESP32 activates the buzzer.
  • The audible alert warns against animal or human intrusion.
  • This improves crop safety and reduces potential damage.

Working Flow

  • ESP32 initializes all sensors, LCD, and output devices on power-up.
  • Soil moisture is read and evaluated.
  • Water pump is automatically turned ON or OFF based on moisture threshold.
  • Temperature and humidity data are collected using the DHT11 sensor.
  • Water tank level is measured using the ultrasonic sensor.
  • LEDs indicate water availability status.
  • PIR sensor detects motion and triggers the buzzer if intrusion occurs.
  • All sensor readings and system states are displayed on the LCD.
  • The entire process repeats continuously in a timed loop for real-time monitoring.
